Contemporary architecture and interior design from Córdoba, Argentina

Abdenur Architects is a multi-generational design studio based in Córdoba, Argentina, specializing in contemporary architecture and bespoke interior design. Our practice spans over six decades, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation.

We create thoughtful, site-specific designs that respond to local context while embracing international design standards. Our work ranges from private residences to commercial spaces, always emphasizing quality, sustainability, and timeless appeal.

Each project begins with careful listening and deep understanding of our clients' vision, resulting in spaces that are both functional and emotionally resonant.
